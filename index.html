How it worksGetting started
01
Create your account

Click Register in the top right corner. Choose a username, enter your email and a password of at least 6 characters.

Your User ID
Once logged in, click your username (top right) to see your User ID. Share it with your captain so they can add you to the team.
02
Create your team

Go to the Teams tab and click + Create Team. Pick a name and a short tag (e.g. CG). You become the team captain.

One team per player
Each account can only belong to one team at a time.
03
Add your players

Each teammate must create their own account first and share their User ID with you. As captain, click Manage Team in the Teams tab and paste each player's User ID.

Captain only
Only the team Owner can add or remove players.
04
Enroll in the tournament

Once your team is ready, click Enroll in Tournament in the Teams tab. The admin will review and approve your request. Once approved you'll appear in the bracket.

Registration must be open
Enrollment only works when the tournament status is OPEN.
